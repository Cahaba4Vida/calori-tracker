Delete Your Account – Aethon Fuel

This page explains how users of Aethon Fuel, operated by Zach Edwards LLC, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please email:

zach@zachedwardsllc.com

Include the email address associated with your account (if applicable) and the phrase: "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verified request, we will permanently delete:
  • Your account profile information
  • Email address (if registered)
  • Meal logs and food entries
  • Uploaded food images
  • Voice input data
  • AI-generated analysis history
  • Subscription status associated with your account

Data Retention

We do not retain personal account data after deletion is completed. Certain transactional records related to payments may be retained by our payment processor (Stripe) for legal, tax, or accounting purposes in accordance with applicable law.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.
