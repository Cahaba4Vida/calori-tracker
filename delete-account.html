This page explains how users of Aethon Fuel, operated by Zach Edwards LLC, can delete their profile and associated in-app data.

Delete Directly Inside the App

If you can still sign in, the fastest option is inside Settings in the app:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to the System tab
  3. Find Delete profile
  4. Type Delete exactly
  5. Tap Delete Profile
This permanently deletes database records tied to your signed-in profile and cannot be undone. If you have an active paid subscription, cancel/manage it first before deleting your profile.

What Data Will Be Deleted

In-app deletion removes database records tied to your signed-in profile, including:

  • Your profile information stored in the app database
  • Email association stored in the app database
  • Meal logs and food entries
  • Weight logs and daily summaries
  • Voice input history, coach history, and related thread data
  • Linked device records associated with your profile
  • AI usage history and related in-app profile settings

What May Not Be Deleted Automatically

  • Payment processor records retained by Stripe for legal, tax, fraud, or accounting reasons
  • Identity-provider records outside the app database unless separately removed by that provider/platform

Processing Time

If you delete inside the app while signed in, the database deletion happens immediately. Manual email requests are typically processed within 7 business days.
