1. Information We Collect

  • Device-based account identifier: When you first use the App, we may automatically create an account associated with your device ID to store usage data and preferences.
  • Email address: If you sign up through Netlify Identity, we collect your email address for authentication.
  • User content: Meal logs, text inputs, food images, and other content you submit.
  • Voice input: If you use voice logging, audio may be processed for transcription and analysis.
  • Subscription data: We receive confirmation of subscription status through Stripe.
  • Technical data: Basic device information and usage diagnostics for service reliability.

2. Camera and Microphone Use

The App may request permission to access your device’s camera and microphone solely to enable:

  • Food image capture for calorie analysis
  • Voice-to-text meal logging

These permissions are optional and can be disabled in your device settings.

3. AI Processing (OpenAI)

Aethon Fuel uses artificial intelligence services provided by OpenAI to analyze food images, generate calorie estimates, and provide coaching responses. When you submit text, images, or voice input, that data may be securely transmitted to OpenAI’s API for processing. OpenAI processes data in accordance with its own privacy and security policies.

4. Payments and Subscriptions

Payments are processed through Stripe. We do not store or process full payment card details. Stripe handles payment information in accordance with its own privacy policy.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
